How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!

Note: This is a mod of 2048, and is a fair deal harder. You may want to try and master the original first.

Warning: 4 tiles will be added each move, with a potential of spawning tiles of any value that is a power of 2 up to the largest tile; however, larger tiles are less likely to spawn than lower ones.

Created by Gabriele Cirulli. Based on 1024 by Veewo Studio and conceptually similar to Threes by Asher Vollmer. Modified by Ryan2144.
